Developer-focused cookie manager with real-time change monitoring, environment profiles, and export formats that developers actually use.

## Features

- View, edit, create, and delete cookies for the current site
- Real-time monitoring — see when cookies change and why
- Environment profiles — save and restore cookie states for dev/staging/prod
- Export to curl, wget, JSON, cookie file, or header string
- Filter and search across all cookies
- Light and dark themes
